## 📖 About Splitleaf

Splitleaf is dedicated to:
- Preserving the rich tradition of **psalm singing**
- Supporting communities and individuals in learning and practicing psalmody
- Providing resources, tools, and collaborative spaces for ongoing development
- Encouraging healthy, focused discussion around the musical and practical aspects of psalm singing

Our GitHub repositories and discussions exist to further this mission.
