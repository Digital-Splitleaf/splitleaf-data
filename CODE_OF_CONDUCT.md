# Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose
This community exists **solely to support, preserve, and encourage the continuing practice of psalm singing worldwide**. All participation should contribute constructively to this purpose.

## 2. Expected Behavior
Members of this community are expected to:

- Keep all discussions **focused on psalm singing**—its practice, preservation, history, technique, teaching, and resources.
- Communicate **respectfully**, including when offering corrections, suggestions, or differing perspectives about psalm‑singing practice.
- Contribute in ways that foster a **welcoming and supportive environment** for beginners and experienced practitioners alike.
- Share information, recordings, materials, or insights that uphold the community’s purpose.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior
To maintain a clear and productive space, the following behaviors are **not permitted**:

- **Theological debates**, doctrinal disputes, or attempts to argue religious positions.
- **Political discussions**, commentary, or advocacy of any kind.
- Posting content unrelated to the practice, preservation, or study of psalm singing.
- Harassment, personal attacks, hostile language, or attempts to provoke conflict.
- Spamming, self‑promotion unrelated to psalm singing, or advertising.

Any posts or comments that violate these expectations may be edited, hidden, or deleted.

## 4. Discussion Guidelines
- Keep questions and discussions **practical, historical, musical, or community‑focused**, not doctrinal.
- Ensure you have permission before sharing recordings or materials.
- Use clear titles and categories (when available) to help others navigate discussions.

## 5. Enforcement
Moderators may take any action necessary to maintain the focus and health of the community, including:

- Issuing warnings  
- Editing or deleting posts or comments  
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation  

Serious or repeated violations may result in removal from the community.

## 6. Reporting Problems
If you notice behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

- Report it using GitHub’s built‑in reporting tools, or  
- Contact a project maintainer or moderator privately, or  
- Email the **Digital Spitleaf team** at **digital-splitleaf@protonmail.com**.

Reports will be handled promptly and discreetly.

## 7. Commitment to a Respectful Community
By participating in this repository or its discussions, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct and help maintain a **focused, respectful forum dedicated to the preservation and practice of psalm singing**.
